Our Story

Mom's Italian Villa was founded in 1962 by Ollie "Mom" Parker. Ollie was a passionate cook who loved to share her recipes with others. She opened the restaurant with the goal of creating a warm and welcoming place where people could enjoy delicious food and good company. Over the years, Mom's Italian Villa has become a beloved fixture in the community, known for its dedication to quality and service.

Our Founder

Ollie "Mom" Parker was the founder of Mom's Italian Villa. She was a passionate cook who loved to share her recipes with others. Ollie opened the restaurant in 1958 and quickly became a beloved figure in the community. She was known for her warm smile and welcoming personality. Ollie's dedication to quality and service set Mom's Italian Villa apart from other restaurants in the area.
